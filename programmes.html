Awareness Programme

The foundation is extensively involved in spreading awareness to combat caste based hierarchy in our country. We have taken multiple initatives in this regard. Following are some of the key highlights

  • We have held multiple press conferences to collaborate with media on this issue. Media, being the fourth pillar of democracy, helped in reaching this to the masses.
  • We used local auto rikshaws to advertise this message to common people.
  • We share the complete memorandum with hon'able member of parliament Kumari Shelja .
Published 2 books to fight Caste in India

The director of Brihati Reforms Foundation Shri Wazir Singh Poonia has published 2 books to spread his message of eradicating the evil of case from nation. One book is published in english Casteless India and the other in hindi Jati Viheen Bharat

Representation to Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment and all MPs

We have written to all the hon'able Members of Parliament as well as to Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment to take up the initiative of eradicating caste from the country on a war footing to make dream of our freedom fighters a reality.
